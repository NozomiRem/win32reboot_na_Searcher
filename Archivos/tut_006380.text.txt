Title - How to Use Photon Blasts
text0001 - A Photon Blast is a powerful attack you can unleash by adding it to your<br>Sub-Palettes.<br><br><br>The Photon Blast Gauge fills up as you do battle, and once it is full, you can<br>unleash a Photon Blast.
text0002 - You can add it to your Sub-Palettes by opening the Main Menu with <swguidetype T_MainMenu> <br>and selecting <yellow>[Sub-Palette]<c> under <yellow>[Gear/Sub-Palette]<c>.
text0003 - When you select the slot you want to set something to, a list will appear.<br>Choose <yellow>Activate Photon Blast<c> to add it.<br><br>Once it is added, close the menu with <%Cancel>, and you will be ready to go.
text0004 - Once the Photon Blast Gauge is full, activate a Photon Blast by moving<br>the cursor with <%SubPaletteRight> or <%SubPaletteLeft>, and selecting it with <%SubPalette>. When using a keyboard,<br>you can activate it directly through the corresponding number key,<br>from 1 through to 0.
