Title - Stats
text0001 - Hit Points (HP) are numbers that correspond to your character's health. They<br>will deplete when you take damage from enemy attacks. When they reach zero,<br>you will become incapacitated. Try to recover HP with a Restasigne when it<br>gets low.
text0002 - Photon Points (PP) are consumed when you use a Photon Art or Technique. It<br>will recover <yellow>over time<c> or as you hit enemies <yellow>with normal attacks.<c>
text0003 - Attack is a fundamental stat that increases the damage you deal. You can raise<br>it by leveling up or enhancing your weapon.
text0004 - Defense is a stat that decreases the damage you receive. You can raise it by<br>leveling up or enhancing your armor.
